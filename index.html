About
The Houston-Galveston Area Council Demographic Explorer shows demographic data from the latest American Community Survey (ACS). The data includes cities, counties, Census Tracts, Census Block Groups, Zip Code, Independent School Districts (ISDs), Congressional Districts, and Opportunity Zones.

Disclaimer: This application is intended to give an overview of the demographics for different boundary types. The forecast results are experimential and preliminary; subject to change and revisions in subsequent versions. This product is for informational purposes and may not have been prepared for or be suitable for legal, engineering, or surveying purposes. It does not represent an on-the-ground survey and represents only the approximate relative location of property boundaries. Mapping may not reflect on the ground conditions. The Houston-Galveston Area Council makes no further claims as to the accuracy or reliability of the data, and neither assumes, nor will accept liability for their use. Use or reproduction of the data without acknowledging the Houston-Galveston Area Council as the source is strictly prohibited.
